The ritual

Three steps, twice a week

01

Apply to damp skin

Squeeze a small amount onto clean, damp fingertips — face and neck.

02

Massage in circles

Work it gently over your skin. You'll feel the texture lifting as you go.

03

Rinse and moisturize

Rinse with warm water, pat dry, and follow with your regular moisturizer.
